    • Bharathakshetra, as the name refers to, is a hub for artistic thrive. Founded in 2001 by spirited visionaries "Dr. Vivekananda Bhaskar" and "Dr. Bhavani Bhaskar", the primary objective is to ensure a high quality classical art is made affordable to all the aspirants. This institute imparts training in Bharatanatyam, Carnatic vocal music, Western dance, Zumba, and classical music on keyboard. Bharathakshetra endeavors to impart classical dance in its purest form.
